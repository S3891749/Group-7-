Heart disease

Details

Smoking, obesity, physical inactivity, stress, a high-salt, high-fat diet, alcohol, high blood pressure, high blood cholesterol, diabetes... are all common factors of cardiovascular disease in adults and middle age. Obesity in children may also lead to an increase in heart disease in youngsters.


Treatment & Prevention

Changes in lifestyle: Eat a low-fat, low-sodium diet, obtain moderate exercise, and avoid smoking and drinking alcohol. Use medicine. In addition to lifestyle changes, doctor may prescribe medications to control heart disease Surgery. If medication doesn't treat your heart disease effectively
