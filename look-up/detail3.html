The most common causes of lung illness are Bacterial Pneumonia, Viral Pneumonia (including Covid-19), Fungal Pneumonia, and Chemical Pneumonia. The majority of pneumonia cases in the community are caused by viruses and bacteria. Pneumonia is a respiratory illness that spreads from person to person through two major routes: direct and indirect.
